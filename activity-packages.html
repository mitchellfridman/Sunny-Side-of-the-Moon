Activities

ZERO G BASKETBALL
Ever dreamt of dunking from the midcourt line?

Come partake in our dome's athletics center's pick up space basketball games with only a fifth of earth's gravity to contend against!

MOON BUGGY
Taming the wild outdoors has a whole new meaning!

Take part in a one of a kind moon rally. After being given a thorough one hour brief, spend the next hour exploring the moon like the pioneer you are!

MOON CHEESE
Your mom used to tell you that the moon was made out cheese when you were young. Now you get to taste it!

During colonisation, we realized that cheese left in a vaccum developed a very unique taste. The rest... is history!

JOINING THE DARK SIDE
No aliens there, we've checked. But why don't you come over and make sure?

The dark side of the moon has been hidden from humanity until now! Embark on our shuttle to explore it for yourself!

GIFT SHOP
What would any resort be without its very own giftshop?

Immortalize your moon stay by buying a replica of Apollo 14's command module made with actual lunar ore!

STORMTROOPER ACADEMY
What? You thought you would go in space and NOT have a laser fight?

Join this tongue-in-cheek recreation of a stormtrooper academy is faithfully recreated from the Star Wars franchise. Your guns are guarranteed to miss their mark!

MOON DOME
Lay your eyes on the eigth wonder of the world!

The "DOME" is the climate controlled bubble you will be living in for the duration of your trip. Join-us for a guided tour of its history.

GREENHOUSE LUNA
Did you know the moon is self-sustaining with its food?

Every weekend, get the chance to visit LUNA, the moon's first every greenhouse, and help our botanists grow lichen used in our terraforming projects!

NOVA LUNA
Leave your symbolic mark by helping terraform the moon!

Terraforming the moon and creating a permanent atmosphere is the engineering challenge of a millenia, and the endeavour of generations. Come plant with us O2 producing lichen and be immortalized in history.

STAGED MOON LANDING SET
The conspiracies were right! This is where it all didn't happen!

A guided visit through the set used to fake the Apollo 11 landing and moon walk. Each entry ticket includes one 5-minute staged video shoot to convince your friends back home that you were indeed on the moon too!

SPACE WALK
A 15-hour training regime culminating in a solo space walk!

Sign your liability waiver and hop in a TCSS, that is, Thrust Controlled Spacesuit. Each day you will partake in 3 hours of rigourous exercises and drills in preparation for an unassisted spacewalk on the flight home!

MOON ROCK
Rocks? Boring. Rocks on the Moon? Cliché. A Chris Hadfield rock concert on the moon? Now we're talking!

Canadian Astronaut extraordinaire Chris Hadfiled has signed a two-year residency at the New LUNA hotel. We guaranteed you haven't lived until you've listened to "Major Tom" while looking down on the earth.

HISTORIC JAVELIN THROW
On Feb, 6, 1971, Ed Mitchell, the sixth man to walk to moon, made history. Can you top it?

Ed Mitchell can be credited as the first man to partake in the Moon Olympics when he threw his spent solar experiment rig as a javelin. Can you beat his record of 18.5 miles?

GLIDE OVER TRANQUILITY
Buzz Aldrin. Neil Armstrong. The Eagle Lunar Landing Module. This is where it all began.

"Houston, Tranquility Base here. The Eagle has landed."

Neil Armstrong

Hop on our hovership and take in a bird's eye view of where it all began.

TYCHO CRATER
Sometimes mistaken as the largest crater on the Moon, it's definitely the brightest!

Affectionatly known as the new crater on the block, this 108 million year old caldera still shines bright from the matter it ejected. Go on a 2 day trip where you get to sample the soil and bungee jump into it!

MOON WEDDING
From the geniuses that brought you honeymoons, we present to you now marriagemoons!

A reception. A banquet. A ceremony. A story as old as time. Book your special day with us as part of the wedding package, or attend wedding rehearsal that are ongoing through the month. An unforgettable experience!
